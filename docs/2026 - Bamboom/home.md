# About the Robot
Our robot for the 2026 season, named **Bamboom**, was designed for the primary scoring method of the *Rebuilt* season.

**Bamboom** remained competitive for the entirety of the season, allowing us to be Captain of Alliance 7 at the *Magnolia Regional* and the Third Team of Alliance 4 at the *Tallahassee Regional*

## What Was the Game?
The game of the 2026 FRC season, *Rebuilt*, had only two methods of scoring.

### Primary Scoring
For the entire duration of the game, you could score 1 point per ball scored into a funnel on your side of the field called the **Hub**.

Your hub would only be scorable for 30 seconds at a time, alternating between the alliances throughout the match. There is no bonus for scoring in the Autonomous period (20 seconds long this year).

### Endgame Climbing
You could also score points during the end of both Autonomous and Teleop by climbing a tower at the edge of your field toward the driver station. 
During Teleop you would earn 10, 20, or 30 points based on the height of your climb. During Autonomous, you earn 15 points regardless of the height.

Most teams disregarded the climbing aspect as it was deemed too much of a time investment for the points rewarded.

## What Did the Robot Do?
It was decided that it was best to focus on shooting balls into the Hub, and that we should be able to intake the balls from the floor rather than from the human feeding station.

Our team ended up having an intake that deploys over the bumper (pivoting down into place) that pushed balls into the bumper, bouncing them up into the main part of the robot where they would be led into the shooter (which is statically positioned).
