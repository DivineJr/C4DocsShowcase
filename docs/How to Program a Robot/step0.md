# Step 0: Some Groundwork
## WPILib!
Just letting you know that everything in this guide is based in some part on the work of the maintainers and programmers of WPILib, the official toolset of FRC. Everything they do is awesome, and programming a robot would be many, many magnitudes harder without the countless hours and lines of code they put in to make our lives easier.

---

You can find the official WPILib documentation, the basis of everything, here:

[Link to WPILib](https://docs.wpilib.org/en/stable/)

A lot of this guide is going to assume you know how to program, at least on a basic level, in Java. It's okay if you don't, there's plenty of resources to learn out there, most notably your mentors at 6184. Ask them, and they can get you started at a basic level, and we can try to work your way up here.
